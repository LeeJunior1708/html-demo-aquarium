Who We Are

The Nexus aquarium was established with the noble mission of preserving marine animals and helping them to grow in numbers to avoid the risk of extinction.

We have different habitats but we are on the same planet

With that noble mission with help from people who love animals in general and lovers of marine animals in particular. We have developed the aquarium on a large scale to help more than 5000 creatures, promising to help people learn more about marine species so that people understand their importance and help people especially Children especially develop a love for animals and the importance of protecting the environment and animals.
