About Us

The mission of preserving marine animals

The Nexus Aquarium is currently nurturing a lot of marine animals, including some rare animals. The construction of this aquarium aims to contribute to the protection and development of marine animals, in addition to spreading the spirit of animal protection to everyone through feeling up close the beauty of the ocean.

Our Values

Nexus is a large aquarium, a common home for many marine animals, including many rare animals, besides, we have a great mission to spread the spirit of environmental protection to the world, especially the younger generation

The dedicated security team always ensures the safety of everyone, especially children
