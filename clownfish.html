Distribution and habitat

Clown fish live in warm waters in Indian Ocean and Pacific, including the Great Barrier Reef reef and the Red Sea. While some species are limited, others are spread. Clown fish live at the bottom of the agricultural waters in the aviation areas with a shallow place or a shallow lagoon. There are no clowns that live in the Atlantic Ocean.
